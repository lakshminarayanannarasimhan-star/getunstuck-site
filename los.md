<!-- DESIGN PRINCIPLES -->
<section class="section-wide">
  <div class="section-label">Seven design principles</div>
  <h2>What holds this system together.</h2>
  <div class="col-3" style="margin-top:2rem;gap:1px;background:var(--border);border:1px solid var(--border);border-radius:var(--radius-md);overflow:hidden;">
    <div class="card" style="border:none;border-radius:0;">
      <div class="num">01 — Installation Over Information</div>
      <p style="color:var(--muted);font-size:.9rem;margin-top:.5rem;">Capabilities are not learned until demonstrated, and not installed until they survive absence.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card" style="border:none;border-radius:0;">
      <div class="num">02 — Practice Over Content</div>
      <p style="color:var(--muted);font-size:.9rem;margin-top:.5rem;">Reading is insufficient. Capability requires drills, protocols, missions, reflection, evidence, and review.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card" style="border:none;border-radius:0;">
      <div class="num">03 — Progressive Stewardship</div>
      <p style="color:var(--muted);font-size:.9rem;margin-top:.5rem;">Leadership develops through increasing spheres of stewardship — from self outward to institutions.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card" style="border:none;border-radius:0;">
      <div class="num">04 — Evidence-Based Advancement</div>
      <p style="color:var(--muted);font-size:.9rem;margin-top:.5rem;">Advancement requires proof. Not attendance. Not quizzes. Not course completion.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card" style="border:none;border-radius:0;">
      <div class="num">05 — Transfer Before Mastery</div>
      <p style="color:var(--muted);font-size:.9rem;margin-top:.5rem;">Capability is not fully installed until it can be transferred to others. Teaching is the test.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card" style="border:none;border-radius:0;">
      <div class="num">06 — Lifelong Development</div>
      <p style="color:var(--muted);font-size:.9rem;margin-top:.5rem;">LOS supports development from emerging leader to institutional steward. There is no graduation.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="card" style="border:none;border-radius:0;grid-column:span 1;">
      <div class="num">07 — The System Practises What It Teaches</div>
      <p style="color:var(--muted);font-size:.9rem;margin-top:.5rem;">LOS is itself subject to renewal, evidence, and transfer. A system that exempts itself from its own laws has already failed Principle 4.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
