<!-- WHY IT EXISTS -->
<section class="section-wide sh-section">
  <div class="sh-block">
    <div class="sh-num">01</div>
    <div class="sh-content">
      <h2 class="sh-heading">Why Get Unstuck exists</h2>
      <p class="sh-body">Most leadership writing stops after the argument. It tells you what to think. It rarely helps you do something different on Monday.</p>
      <p class="sh-body">Get Unstuck is built around a different premise: reading is not installation. An idea that stays in your head is not yet useful. The platform is designed to take ideas all the way to capability — through articles that build the argument, drills that practice it, and laws that compress it into something you can carry.</p>
      <p class="sh-body">Everything here is drawn from 25 years of building organisations from the inside. Not observed. Carried. The failures informed the theories as much as the successes.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
